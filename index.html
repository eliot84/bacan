About Us

Bacán is the premiere peruvian cuisine restaurant in Miami located on the rooftop of the Brickell Arch. The restaurant is dedicated to providing authentic world class Peruvian cuisine at reasonable prices. Our promise is to always use fresh ingredients and to never substitute quality. We look forward to welcoming you to our home away from peruvian home.
